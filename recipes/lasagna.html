Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish that is hearty and satisfying. Perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
